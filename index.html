  So What Do We Do ?

1947 is a very special year, not just to Indians, but to people living in every nook and corner of the world. It was the year when ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) was founded. ACM Student Chapter have been established in various institutions across the world since then. SVCE's ACM Student Chapter is one of the most well reputed associations both within and outside of the institution.Although it wasn't a part of the college when the college was first founded, it is now a permanent and integral part of SVCE which has achieved great acclaim and laurels throughout the years it has been active. Even though we are quite few in number, we strongly believe that quality is more important than quantity.

Organizing technical events intertwined with loads of fun filled challenges is our forte. Our wide variety of events boost both 'competing by learning' as well as 'learning by competing'! Participating in our events helps you take a step towards success, and thereby guides you to make your path towards success an interesting one rather than a difficult one. Things cannot get better than this!'

  Events

Inauguration

25th July, 2017

The 25th of July, 2017, marked the inauguration of the ACM SVCE Student Chapter for the academic year 2017 - 2018. The Chief Guest for the event was Mr Rajesh Padmanabhan, Founder and CEO of NFN Labs, who enthused the audience with a realistic but motivating outlook on life, technology and entrepreneurial spirit. Additionally, the members of last year's chapter were felicitated and were encouraged to continue working with the current chapter.

Game Of Codes

4th August, 2017

The aim was to provide student-sourced guidance to other students on attaining maximum success in placement interviews. The event kick started with a mock coding round, followed by an interactive session where students who had already been offered jobs at companies such as Dell shared their valuable experience in interviews and answers to commonly asked programming questions. The event was a success, with each participant getting a confidence boost!

  Know Who Made Us What We Are

Founder

Currently a second year Ph.D. student at University of Montreal, Sharat Chandar was part of the class of 2012 of the Department of CSE, SVCE. Under the guidance of Dr.Susan Elias, former Head of Department (CSE), Sharat formed and the SVCE-ACM Student Chapter and was instrumental in getting the chapter officially chartered by ACM New York on 26th August 2011. He served as the Chairman of the chapter for the year 2011-12 and one of his major accomplishments include successfully organizing the National Level Programming Contest (NLPC), a nation-wide coding contest, during the year 2011.Before joining University of Montreal, Sharat was a Research Software Engineer at IBM India Research Lab between the period August 2014 - May 2015. Sharat did his MS by Research at Indian Institute of Technology Madras where he was working with Dr.Balaraman Ravindran.
